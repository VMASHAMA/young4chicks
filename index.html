About Young4ChickS

Our Mission

Founded by a youth Member of Parliament, Young4ChickS facilitates young urban dwellers to stimulate wealth creation and money economy involvement through poultry farming.

What We Offer

  • Quality layer and broiler chicks (local and exotic)
  • Fixed pricing at UGx 1,650 per chick
  • 2 bags of feed per farmer (payable after 2 months)
  • Support for both starter and returning farmers

Eligibility

  • Youth aged 18-30 years
  • Must be registered with recommender details
  • Starter farmers: Maximum 100 chicks
  • Returning farmers: Maximum 500 chicks
  • Requests allowed once every 4 months
